Our Features

19-Inch Size

The large 19-inch diameter of the ring light provides wide and even illumination, reducing shadows and ensuring a flattering light for your face and surroundings.

3 Phone Holders

The included phone holders allow you to mount up to three phones at once, making it easy to stream, record, or video call from multiple angles or platforms.

Advanced Control Options

The ring light features 2.4G wireless control, smooth dimming down to 1%, and an LCD/touchscreen for intuitive adjustment of brightness and color temperature, enhancing user convenience and flexibility.
