Our Features

360° Coverage

The TP-Link Tapo C200 offers 360° panoramic coverage through its pan-and-tilt functionality. This allows the camera to rotate horizontally and vertically, providing a comprehensive view of the entire room without blind spots. Benefits: The ability to cover the full room with a single camera means you don't need multiple cameras to monitor different areas. This is especially useful for maintaining a clear and continuous view of your space, making it easier to keep an eye on everything happening within the room.

Real-Time Notifications

The Tapo C200 sends real-time notifications to your smartphone when it detects motion or unusual activity. These alerts are generated through its advanced motion detection technology, which can distinguish between different types of movement. Unique Aspects and Advantages: This feature ensures that you are promptly informed of any activity, allowing you to respond quickly if needed. The ability to receive instant notifications enhances your security by keeping you updated even when you are away from home.

Night Vision

The camera is equipped with infrared night vision capabilities, allowing it to capture clear video footage even in complete darkness. Use Cases and Benefits: Night vision is crucial for monitoring your home around the clock, providing security and peace of mind regardless of the time of day. This feature ensures that the camera continues to function effectively during the night, making it easier to track any nighttime activity.
